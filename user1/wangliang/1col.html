March 8, 2006

The new design seems to be up and running. I have been pondering what to name this template while putting it up as an Open Source design. Well, I couldn't think of anything creative, so I went with Antique_Modern. There is a 2 column layout for most pages and a 1 column layout (which you see here) for alternate pages. Take a look around, and enjoy!
