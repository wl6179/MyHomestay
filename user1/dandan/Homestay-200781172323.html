About Us

MHA (Myhomestaystay Agency)  is based in Beijing China. We offer visitors and students from around the globe a perfect chance to experience the Chinese lifestyle and culture. We have several styles of homestaystays; conventional, holiday and career homestaystays.

The agency will act as your agent, and provide you with caring host families in a secure, safe and happy environment. We will give you information about your host family before booking. We will also secure your booking with the host family. Our representatives provide airport pickup, initial orientation and introduction to host family.
