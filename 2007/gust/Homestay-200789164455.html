 住家外教--海淀-Andrew

Andrew

Dear Homestay Provider,

My name is Andrew McCallum. I am a 25 years old Canadian and have lived in Beijing for two years. When I first arrived in China, I spent almost 9 months studying Chinese at 南开大学 and 黑龙江大学. I was then hired at The Balloch Group, which is a Canadian investment and consulting firm headed by the former Canadian Ambassodor to China, Howard Balloch. After working at the Balloch Group for more than a year, I resigned in order to study to become a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and improve my Chinese. I plan to return to work in 4 months to a year, after I have completed my CFA training in China.

I am currently looking for a homestay that will provide a 100% Chinese-speaking environment. I am not interested in speaking or teaching any English. I am currently at五道口, and would be prefer to have a homestay not too far from there. My reasons for wanting a homestay is not only to improve my Chinese, but also to learn more about Chinese culture and way of living.  

I am a very easy person to get along with, I am not loud and am very polite. I do not like to go out late at night. I think that any family would really like me because of my positive attitude.

I hope I can find an appropriate homestay.

Yours Sincerely,

Andrew
