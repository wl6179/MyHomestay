 住家外教--海淀-Darcy


 

First Name:  Darcy  

Gender:  Female

Birthday :  06/10/1988

First language?  English

Level of Chinese:  Beginner

Have you ever lived away from your home/family? Where and How long?

Yes, during college.  Eight months

When do you plan to start homestay? 
Early May 2007

How long would you plan to stay with a family? 
Five to Six months C the duration of my stay                  

 

Do you prefer a family that smokes:

Doesn’t Matter

Do you drink alcohol? Yes

Do you mind a family with children? No

In China, many people have household pets. Do you have an allergy to animals?

No

 

What do you enjoy doing at the weekends?

Go out, visit neighborhoods I don’t know, visit Museums, sight see, get to know the city, read                                                                                  

                                                                                 

 

Please describe in your own words your reasons for wanting to do a homestay:

I plan studying anthropology and would love this opportunity to gain further insight into Chinese culture.  Furthermore, I greatly desire to improve my Chinese.  I would also love to meet a completely new family and get to know their habits and customs. 

 

What are your hobbies and interests?

Anthropology, Art history, reading, exercising (gym, sports scare !me)                                                                                  

Do you play a musical instrument:

Yes C Piano and Cello

 

Please describe your educational background:  I am a high school graduate and an undergraduate at the University of Chicago.  I am currently a first year student.  I have decided to take a year off to improve my Chinese (mainly) and learn from a new country.

The last school you studied: University of Chicago

Please describe your previous work experience: I have experience with customer service.  I also worked with a psychologist, helping him on a long term research project he was studying at my school.
