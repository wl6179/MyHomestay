 住家外教--海淀-Margarita-2007.8

Margarita Vasileva Yanachkova

 

PERSONAL INFORMATION:

single; born 17.02. 1977

 

OBJECTIVE:

To look for new ideas to improve my teaching practice. To widen my teaching methods, specially with teenage students. As well as broaden my cross cultural experience.

 

PROFILE:

Highly energetic, creative and enthusiastic English language teacher, specializing in primary school teaching. Dedicated to providing the best education and care to primary school children aged 6-12 years.

 

EXPERIENCE:

7thSeptember 2005 C present C English Language Teacher, I and II grade, Private English Language School “St. Sofia”

・        full Ctime ELT to primary school children (6-9 years old). Teaching them reading and writing, as well as grammar, listening and communication.

・        Preparing them for the Cambridge Flyers Exam

・        Devising lessons, games, tests and extra teaching materials. Reviewing assignments and correcting all homework and writing papers with an emphasis on providing feedback and individual homework.

・        Tutoring children with temporally difficulties after school

・        Providing written reports twice a month to the parents on the child’s progress and behavior.

1stOctober 2006 C present C English Language Teacher,English Language School “Britanica”

・        ELT to evening course groups aged 9-11.

・        Preparing them for the Cambridge Starters Exam

・        Reviewing assignments and correcting all homework and writing projects with an emphasis on providing feedback to each child.

・        Tutored individually a special need child (VYL). Working to meet its needs by trying multiple intelligence approach and a lot of hands-on-activities

1st January 2002 C September 2005  C English Language Teacher, I and II grade, Private Primary English Language School “Duga

・        full Ctime ELT to primary school children (6-8 years old). Teaching them reading and writing, as well as grammar, listening and communication. Using a lot of versatile approaches, project work, games, etc.

・        Preparing them for the Chichester College Exams ( First steps, Primary and Preliminary levels)

・        Preparing all the materials needed for the lessons, all the extra materials and didactic games.

・        Providing weekly written reports to the parents on the child’s progress and behavior.

EDUCATION:

・        February 2002 C February 2006 C Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski’, PhD research student in the field of Primary School Pedagogy, full-time

・        November 2000 C June 2004 C Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski’, Master degree in Aural and Verbal Rehabilitation, full-time

・        October 1999 C June 2004 - Sofia University ‘St. Kliment Ohridski’, Bachelor of Pedagogy of Mentally Retarded Children, part-time

・         January C July 2000 - 6-month practice teaching (Primary School Pedagogy) in KATHO RENO, Belgium as an Erasmus  student

・        October 1996 C September2000 C Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski’, Bachelor of Primary School Pedagogy; specialization C Fine Arts, full-time

 

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:

・        Private home tutoring. Working with different level children since the autumn of 2003. Consecutively preparing them for the Chichester College Exams and Pitman Exams (Basic, Elementary and Intermediate).

・        During the summer of 2003 worked as a camp counsellor (Camp America) in a Girl Scout camp in Vermont, USA. Living, working and playing with the campers (aged 8-12) 24 hours a day.

・        3rd July -14th July2006 undertook a Teacher Refresher Course for Teaching English to Primary School students in Lake School of English, Oxford (Comenius grant)

・        Monthly participation in the Oxford ELT seminars held in Sofia

・        Taking part in a voluntary organization for helping orphan children by collecting clothes, books and toys

・        Publications, seminar presentations and workshops in the fields of Primary School Pedagogy (project work) and Special Need Children Pedagogy (comparing educational systems Bulgaria - Belgium)

 

AWARDS RECEIVED:

・        July 2006 C Comenius grant C 2 week teacher training course in England

・        January C July 2000 C Erasmus grant C exchange program in Belgium

・        February 2002 C February 2006 C PhD government grant

・        1996 C 2000 C  every year won a university government scholarship for achievements

・        1992 C 1996 C every term won a high school government scholarship for achievements

・        was invited to join MENSA Bulgaria

・        Considered the best ELT in Duga Private Primary Language School. The principle and the parents invited me to continue teaching their children after the 1st and 2nd grade due to the high motivation and success my pupils had. After leaving the school was contacted by parents as a private tutor.

 

PERSONAL DETAILS:

Constantly trying to find new and better ways to teach Was considered for a teaching position with the VSO. Enjoying reading (especially historical novels, children’s literature and fantasy), dancing, music and hiking with friends. Writing poetry.

・        Languages - English, Russian (personal use) ,Deutsche and Dutch (basic)

・        Computers - Word, Windows, Internet, Excel, Power Point and Corel Draw
